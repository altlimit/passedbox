Credit Expiration

When enabled, the encrypted key share is automatically released when your credits run out. Simply don't buy more to trigger the switch. Purchase additional credits anytime to keep it held.

Keep-Alive Check-In

When enabled, you must periodically check in via calendar reminders or web push notifications. Miss your check-ins and the share is released automatically.

Enable either trigger or both — they work independently.
